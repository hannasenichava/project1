Portuguese Sangria


Ingredients:

  • Wine (1Lt.)
  • raspberries (250gr)
  • Cinamon
  • Ice


Directions:

  1. Mix the raspberries with the cinamon
  2. Merge the misture with the wine and shake it
  3. Add the ice right before it goes to be consume
  4. Enjoy!
